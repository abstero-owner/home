Sales Operations~15 hrs/week saved

Cold email pipeline

Prospect research, personalized openers, scheduled sends, and reply tagging — so your team spends time on conversations, not mail merge.

The problem

Outbound eats half the sales team's week: finding leads, writing one-off openers, hitting send, then sorting replies into interested / not now / wrong person / unsubscribe. Most of that is repetitive — and easy to get wrong at scale.

How it works

  1. 01

    Pull prospects from your list

    HubSpot segment, CSV upload, or Airtable view — however you store targets today.

  2. 02

    Research and draft

    AI pulls company news, LinkedIn activity, or site copy and writes a short opener tied to something real.

  3. 03

    Send on schedule

    Messages go out through your sending tool with daily caps and timezone spacing you control.

  4. 04

    Classify replies

    Responses get tagged in HubSpot — interested, timing, wrong person, unsubscribe — and hot replies ping Slack.

Integrations

What you get

  • Personalized openers without your reps writing each one by hand
  • Send limits and warmup rules baked in — not bolted on after a domain gets flagged
  • CRM stays clean: every reply classified, no inbox archaeology
