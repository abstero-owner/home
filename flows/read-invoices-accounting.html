Customer Operations~8 hrs/week saved

Read invoices → accounting

Invoice PDFs from your inbox get parsed, categorized, and posted to Xero or QuickBooks — without someone opening every attachment and re-typing line items.

The problem

Your bookkeeper or ops person spends hours each week downloading invoice attachments, copying amounts and vendor names into a spreadsheet or accounting tool, and chasing mismatches when a PDF format changes. That work doesn't require judgment — it requires patience you're paying too much for.

How it works

  1. 01

    Watch your invoice inbox

    The flow monitors a dedicated Gmail label or forwarding address where suppliers send invoices.

  2. 02

    Extract line items

    AI reads PDFs and image attachments — vendor, date, amounts, tax, PO numbers — and normalizes them into a consistent structure.

  3. 03

    Match to your vendor rules

    Known vendors map to the right expense categories and GL codes. New vendors get flagged once for your approval, then remembered.

  4. 04

    Post to accounting

    Validated entries sync to Xero, QuickBooks, or a Google Sheet staging tab — whichever you already use.

  5. 05

    Flag exceptions

    Duplicates, missing fields, or amounts over your threshold go to a Slack channel or email — not silently skipped.

Integrations

Gmail Google Sheets Xero QuickBooks Slack

What you get

  • Invoices processed within minutes of arrival, not end-of-week batch runs
  • A clear exception queue instead of surprises at month-end close
  • Vendor and category rules that improve as you approve edge cases
  • Audit trail: original attachment linked to every posted entry
  • Hosted, monitored, and maintained by us — you don't touch the infrastructure
