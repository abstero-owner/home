How it works

From boilerplate flow to your production system in three weeks.

No retainers, no black boxes. We start from a battle-tested flow that already does the job, then fork it into a private copy that speaks your data, your tone, your stack.

Reasonable doubts

Honest answers to the questions
buyers always ask second.

Are we just paying for a template?

No. The library is the starting point — the saving is in the weeks we don't have to spend on plumbing. Every deploy is forked into your private workspace, rewired around your data, and tested against your real edge cases. The deliverable is a flow that runs only for you.

What happens if Abstero disappears tomorrow?

The flow keeps running. You own the source, the prompts, the credentials, and (if you chose self-hosted) the runtime. n8n is open-source; the scripts are plain Python or TypeScript. Any competent engineer can pick it up — that's the contract.

Does the AI hallucinate on customer-facing work?

Every customer-facing flow ships with guardrails: source citations, allow-lists, schema validation, and a human approval step where the cost of being wrong is real. We will tell you in the audit which jobs are safe to automate fully and which should stay draft-only.

How much engineering capacity do we need on our side?

For a typical deploy: one operator who knows the process, two or three half-hour calls, and a single afternoon to review documentation. No engineer needed unless you want the flow self-hosted, in which case our team works directly with yours.

What about data privacy and SOC 2 / GDPR?

Self-hosted flows never let your data leave your tenant. Managed flows run in EU regions by default with DPA, sub-processor list and SOC 2-aligned controls. We don't train on your data, and we delete artefacts on request — in writing.

What's the price?

Fixed-fee per flow: typically $2,400 — $11,000 depending on the number of integrations and the depth of customisation. The audit call is free and you leave it with a written number. No retainer, no per-seat pricing, no scope creep invoices.
